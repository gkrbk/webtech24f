Lebron James Fanpage

The objective of my website:

The objective of my project's website will be to showcase the highlights, career, and achievements of the NBA player Lebron James. Lebron James is many of my friends' and my own personal favorite basketball player ever. We often watch highlights and talk about the basketball career of Lebron, who we believe is the best basketball player of all time. This website will be a place for the Lebron James enthusiast to view his most impressive highlights, learn about his life, learn about his career statistics, learn about the sport of basketball in the era in which he played, and hear about what other NBA legends think of Lebron. To somebody new to the sport of basketball, either as a player or watcher, this website will serve as a place to learn all about the best player to ever play.

The content of each page:

On the home page of the website, users will be able to read a brief overview about Lebron James and the NBA along with an option to learn more. Users will also be shown some of his most impressive highlights with an option to watch more. users will also be shown his most recent stats with an option to view more in depth statistics of his various years in the nba and his competitors stats as well. Users will be able to view what some of his competitors and retired NBA legends think of Lebron with an option to view more.

the highlights page will display his most impressive highlights broken into different categories: the highlights displaying the most impressive athleticism and basketball ability and the highlights displaying the plays that held the most impact in terms of altering NBA history, such as game winning blocks to win a championship.

The About Lebron page will discuss the history of this legendary basketball player along with interesting facts, such as him being ranked as the #1 football player from the state of ohio before he began playing basketball competitively.

The Statistics page will display many stats about the different years he has played in the nba along with his playoff stats in the years that his team made the playoffs.

The history of basketball page will discuss the state of the NBA in which he played along with information about the era of the NBA that existed before he entered.

The NBA Legends opinions page will display info, potentially interview clips, of NBA legends talking about their views of lebron and their thoughts and reasons why he is the best to ever play.

the about me page will talk about my opinions about this player and about my appreciation for the sport of basketball.

the contact us page will allow users to see how they can contact me.

the sitemap page will allow users to navigate to a specific page.

the credits page will sum up the entire website.
