How do I know if I need a new HVAC system?

If your current system struggles to maintain temperature, requires frequent repairs, or leads to high energy bills, it may be time for a replacement. Our experts assess your needs and recommend the best energy-efficient solution for your home.

What makes OHL Air Conditioning's HVAC installation services different?

We follow a meticulous process that includes assessing your home's needs, selecting the right unit size, and ensuring proper placement. Our team prioritizes energy efficiency, durability, and long-term comfort while maintaining transparent pricing.

How do you determine the right HVAC unit size for my home?

We perform a thorough load calculation considering your home's square footage, insulation, and layout. Proper sizing prevents inefficiencies, ensuring your system operates efficiently without overworking or short cycling.

Will I notice immediate benefits after a new HVAC installation?

Yes! A professionally installed system improves energy efficiency, delivers consistent cooling, and reduces your utility bills. You'll also experience better air quality and a more comfortable indoor environment.

How long does an HVAC installation take?

Most installations are completed within a single day, depending on the complexity of the project. We arrive on time, work efficiently, and leave your home clean while ensuring everything is properly tested and calibrated.

Does OHL Air Conditioning offer warranties on new HVAC installations?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. We ensure your system is installed correctly and offer support to keep it running smoothly for years to come.
