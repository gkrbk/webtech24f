Proactive AC Maintenance in Delray Beach, FL, for Lasting Comfort and Efficiency.

Prioritizing Your Comfort: Just like a car needs routine checkups for optimal performance and longevity, your AC system thrives on consistent AC Maintenance in Delray Beach FL. Skipping it can lead to unexpected breakdowns, increased energy costs, and compromised indoor air quality. A typical residential air conditioner can produce roughly 30 gallons of water per day and run 10–15 hours daily — conditions that naturally create slime and buildup in the drain line and pan beneath the unit. While homeowners can do their best to keep these areas clean, professional maintenance is essential to prevent issues and keep your system running at its best.

What Our Maintenance Service Includes:

  • Thorough Electrical Checks: We examine all electrical components to ensure they're functioning safely and efficiently.
  • Meticulous Cleaning: Our team carefully wipes down wires, clears drain lines to prevent clogs, and removes debris from coils.
  • Exterior Inspection: We inspect your condenser unit, removing leaves or any obstructions that could reduce airflow.
  • Ductwork Assessment: We check for leaks in your ductwork that may waste energy and affect airflow.
  • Refrigerant Level Check: We verify refrigerant levels to maintain optimal system performance and efficiency.

Your Comfort is Our Priority: At OHL Air Conditioning, we believe proactive maintenance is the key to preventing future problems and ensuring year-round comfort. Once we finish your maintenance visit, we'll discuss any potential concerns and provide honest recommendations to keep your system running smoothly.

By choosing OHL Air Conditioning for your AC maintenance, you're investing in the long-term health and efficiency of your system. Serving Delray Beach, FL and surrounding areas, OHL Air Conditioning is here to help you stay comfortable all year long.

How often should I schedule AC maintenance?

We recommend professional AC maintenance at least once a year, ideally in the spring before the cooling season begins. Regular maintenance helps prevent breakdowns and keeps your system running efficiently.

What makes OHL Air Conditioning's maintenance services different?

We provide a comprehensive inspection, cleaning, and tune-up of your system. Our team checks all components, ensures optimal performance, and offers honest recommendations to keep your system healthy year-round.

What does a typical maintenance visit include?

Our maintenance includes cleaning coils, checking refrigerant, tightening electrical connections, lubricating moving parts, and testing system performance. We also look for potential issues before they become major problems.

Will I notice immediate benefits after maintenance?

Yes! Maintenance improves energy efficiency, delivers more consistent comfort, and can reduce your utility bills. You'll also enjoy better air quality and peace of mind.

How long does a maintenance visit take?

Most maintenance visits are completed within 1–2 hours, depending on your system. We arrive on time, work efficiently, and leave your home clean while ensuring everything is properly tested and calibrated.

Does OHL Air Conditioning offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer affordable maintenance plans to keep your system running smoothly year-round. Ask us about our plans when you schedule your next visit.
