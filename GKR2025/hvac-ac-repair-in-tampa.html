Signs that your air conditioning might need a repair:

  • Warm Air Blowing from Vents: A clear indicator your AC isn’t cooling properly.
  • Strange Noises: Rattling, banging, squealing, or hissing can signal serious issues.
  • Weak Airflow: Significant decreases in airflow may point to clogged filters, leaks, or other problems.
  • High Energy Bills: A sudden spike in costs without increased usage can mean your unit is working too hard.
  • Inconsistent Cooling: Some rooms being warmer or cooler could indicate duct or airflow concerns.
  • Frequent Cycling: Rapid on-and-off cycles might mean your AC is struggling to maintain the set temperature.
  • Water Leaks: Leaks around the unit suggest refrigerant or drainage issues.
  • Burning Smell: Electrical odors must be addressed immediately to prevent damage or danger.
  • Age of the Unit: Systems over 10 years old may need more frequent repairs or a complete replacement.
  • Thermostat Issues: A thermostat that won’t respond or maintain temperature could be part of the problem.

How do I know if my air conditioner needs repair?

Common signs include warm air blowing from vents, weak airflow, frequent cycling, strange noises, or sudden increases in energy bills. If you experience any of these issues, it's best to have a professional diagnose the problem before it worsens.

What types of AC problems does NextGen Heating & Cooling repair?

We handle refrigerant leaks, electrical failures, thermostat malfunctions, weak airflow, frozen coils, drainage issues, and more. Our expert technicians use advanced diagnostics to quickly identify and resolve your AC issues.

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

This issue can be caused by low refrigerant levels, a faulty compressor, clogged filters, or thermostat malfunctions. We’ll inspect your system and restore proper cooling efficiency.

What should I do if my AC is making strange noises?

Rattling, banging, or hissing noises can indicate loose components, motor issues, or refrigerant leaks. It’s important to address these noises promptly to avoid further damage.

Why is my energy bill suddenly higher?

If your energy costs have spiked without increased usage, your AC may be working harder due to clogged filters, refrigerant leaks, or inefficient components. A timely repair can restore efficiency and lower your costs.

How quickly can NextGen respond to repair requests?
