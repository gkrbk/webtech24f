What types of thermostats are available?

There are three main types of thermostats: manual, programmable, and smart. Manual thermostats require manual adjustments, programmable models allow temperature scheduling, and smart thermostats offer remote control and automation via Wi-Fi.

How can a smart thermostat help me save energy?

Smart thermostats learn your habits, adjust temperatures automatically, and can be controlled remotely. Features like geofencing and energy reports help reduce waste, leading to lower energy bills.

Can I install a new thermostat myself?

While basic models may be DIY-friendly, advanced programmable or smart thermostats require proper wiring and setup. Our professional installation ensures compatibility and seamless operation.

Do smart thermostats work with all HVAC systems?

Most modern smart thermostats are compatible with standard HVAC systems, but some older or multi-stage systems may require additional wiring or adapters. We’ll assess your system and recommend the best option.

How do programmable thermostats improve efficiency?

Programmable thermostats allow you to set heating and cooling schedules, so your system runs only when needed. This helps reduce energy consumption without sacrificing comfort.

Can I control my thermostat remotely?

Yes! Smart thermostats connect to Wi-Fi, allowing you to adjust settings from your phone, tablet, or voice assistant. This ensures your home is always at the ideal temperature, even when you're away.
