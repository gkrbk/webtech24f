Expert Diagnostics & Repair

Common AC Problems We Fix in Catoosa

Whether your system is making strange noises, blowing warm air, or not turning on at all, we have the experience to find and fix the issue fast. We handle all common air conditioning problems to restore comfort to your Catoosa home.

AC Not Turning On

A system that won't start could have a tripped breaker, faulty thermostat, or a bad capacitor. We trace the problem to its source and safely restore operation.

Blowing Warm Air

If the air from your vents isn't cold, it may be due to low refrigerant, dirty coils, or a compressor issue. We diagnose the cause and recommend the right fix.

Strange Noises or Smells

Rattling, buzzing, or musty odors often signal issues with fans, motors, or drainage. We pinpoint the source and make the necessary repairs.

Refrigerant Leaks

Low refrigerant impacts cooling and can harm your compressor. We're certified to find and fix leaks, then recharge the system correctly.

Thermostat Issues

A faulty thermostat can cause short cycling or prevent the AC from starting. We can recalibrate, repair, or replace it for accurate control.

Water Leaks

Water pooling around your indoor unit is often a clogged condensate drain. We clear the blockage and check for underlying issues to prevent damage.
