Project Overview

At Advanced AC & TV, we take pride in delivering reliable and efficient air conditioning repair and installation solutions to both residential and commercial clients across Queens, NY. One of our recent projects highlights our expertise in residential HVAC systems for a local property.

This project involved upgrading a Friedrich console-style indoor air conditioning unit to provide optimal heating and cooling for an open-concept living space. Designed to meet the specific needs of a single-level condo, this system ensures a comfortable, energy-efficient, and fully modernized environment for the homeowner.

We began by conducting a detailed assessment of the space’s layout and energy requirements. Working closely with the client, our team ensured that every element of the design was optimized for performance, efficiency, and ease of use. The centerpiece of this project is a floor-mounted console unit from Friedrich with advanced temperature control, paired with a sleek design to blend seamlessly with the condo’s modern aesthetic. This system provides consistent airflow while maintaining quiet operation, ensuring a more relaxing and pleasant living experience.

Our technicians also focused on maximizing the system’s energy efficiency. We installed insulated ducting to minimize heat loss, reduce energy consumption, and maintain steady performance year-round. By integrating the upgraded unit with the condo’s existing electrical system, we achieved a seamless and highly effective solution tailored to the client’s needs.

As a full-service HVAC company, we don’t just install systems; we also educate our clients on how to maintain them. For this project, we provided a comprehensive walkthrough of the unit’s operation, including filter replacement tips and energy-saving recommendations. By emphasizing safety and education, we empower our clients to enjoy long-term performance and peace of mind.

At Advanced AC & TV, we understand the unique needs of homes and businesses in Queens. This project demonstrates our ability to customize solutions for specific spaces while delivering exceptional customer service.

This project is a testament to our commitment to quality and client satisfaction. From the initial consultation to the final installation, our team worked with precision, expertise, and a deep understanding of the client’s vision.
