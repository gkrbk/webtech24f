Protecting Your Investment: Just like a car needs routine oil changes, your HVAC system requires consistent HVAC Maintenance in Oklahoma City to run reliably. Skipping it can lead to unexpected breakdowns, higher energy bills, and poor air quality. More importantly, most manufacturers require annual maintenance to keep your equipment warranty valid. Our subscription-based plans make this essential service simple and affordable, ensuring your system is always in top condition.

What Our Biannual Tune-Ups Include:

  • Comprehensive Safety & Electrical Checks: We inspect all wiring, test safety controls, and tighten electrical connections.
  • Meticulous Cleaning: Our team thoroughly cleans indoor/outdoor coils, clears drain lines to prevent clogs, and changes standard filters.
  • Mechanical Component Inspection: We lubricate motors, check belts for wear, and ensure all moving parts are in good condition.
  • Ductwork & Airflow Assessment: We check for obvious leaks in your ductwork that can waste energy and reduce comfort.
  • System Performance Calibration: We verify refrigerant levels and test the full system cycle to ensure peak performance and efficiency.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?

For optimal performance and to keep your manufacturer's warranty valid, we recommend two visits per year: one for your air conditioner in the spring and one for your furnace in the fall. This proactive approach is the best way to prevent breakdowns during extreme weather.

What are the benefits of joining a maintenance plan?

Our maintenance plan members receive significant value beyond the two annual tune-ups. Benefits include priority scheduling, discounts on any needed repairs (up to 15% off), and free service calls, making it the most cost-effective way to manage your HVAC system.

What does a typical maintenance visit include?

Our comprehensive tune-up includes cleaning indoor and outdoor coils, checking refrigerant levels, testing safety controls, tightening electrical connections, and calibrating your system for peak efficiency. We provide a detailed report of your system's health after each visit.

Will I notice immediate benefits after maintenance?

Yes. A professional tune-up can restore lost efficiency, which helps lower your monthly energy bills. You'll also gain peace of mind knowing your system has been professionally inspected and is prepared for the upcoming season.

How long does a maintenance visit take?

A thorough maintenance visit for one system typically takes between 60 and 90 minutes. Our technicians are efficient and respectful of your time and property, ensuring a smooth and professional service experience.

Does regular maintenance really extend my HVAC system's life?

Absolutely. Just like regular oil changes for a car, professional maintenance reduces wear and tear on critical components. This not only prevents the majority of unexpected breakdowns but is proven to help your system last for years longer.
