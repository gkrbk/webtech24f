Project Overview

At Cooper Heat & Air, we specialize in providing customized heating and cooling solutions for residential and commercial clients in Oklahoma City, OK. One of our recent projects highlights our expertise in residential HVAC installations and furnace upgrades for a local homeowner.

This project involved installing a new air conditioning unit and upgrading the existing furnace system. The client required a solution that would improve energy efficiency, enhance home comfort, and provide reliable performance year-round.

The installation began with setting up a high-efficiency outdoor condenser unit. Our technicians carefully evaluated the property's outdoor space to ensure optimal placement. This placement not only maximized cooling efficiency but also minimized noise levels, making it ideal for a residential environment. We ensured that all connections were properly insulated and secured to guarantee a durable and effective setup.

Inside the home, we focused on upgrading the furnace system located in the attic. Our team removed the outdated unit and replaced it with a modern, energy-efficient furnace. The new system was integrated with the existing ductwork, ensuring consistent and effective heating throughout the home. We also inspected and adjusted the insulation in the attic to prevent energy loss, which significantly enhances overall efficiency and reduces utility costs for the homeowner.

Throughout the installation process, we prioritized safety and quality. All electrical and gas connections were carefully tested to meet safety standards, and we provided the client with a detailed demonstration of how to operate and maintain their new HVAC system.

By completing this project, the homeowner now enjoys:

  • Improved energy efficiency, reducing monthly utility bills.
  • Enhanced indoor comfort with consistent heating and cooling year-round.
  • A quieter, more reliable system that blends seamlessly into their home.

This project reflects our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. At Cooper Heat & Air, we understand that every home is unique, which is why we work closely with our clients to deliver tailored solutions that meet their specific needs.
