We evaluate your home and cooling needs to recommend the appropriate capacity and efficiency so you get comfort, reliability, and value—without oversizing.

Ductwork Evaluation & Airflow

We check supply and return paths, airflow, and basic sealing needs, then advise on any improvements that help your new system perform as intended.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions About AC Installation in Charlotte, Answered

Contact Us

How much does an AC installation cost in Charlotte?

Price depends on home size, efficiency rating, equipment type, and any ductwork or electrical updates. We perform a home assessment and present a clear, upfront price before any work begins.

How long does a typical AC installation take?

Most standard replacements are completed in a single day. Projects that include duct changes or electrical upgrades can take longer. We schedule arrival, coordinate permits when required, and clean up before we leave.

Do you handle permits and code compliance?

Yes. We coordinate permits where required and complete the installation to local code. If an inspection is required, we help schedule it and make sure the paperwork is in order.

Which AC brands do you install?

We install systems from major manufacturers and match equipment to your home and budget. Brands include Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Goodman, Rheem, Bryant, Daikin, Amana, Mitsubishi Electric, and others.

What size AC do I need for my home?

Right sizing is critical for comfort and efficiency. We evaluate your home and existing ductwork to recommend the appropriate capacity so you avoid short cycling and uneven cooling.

What warranties come with a new AC system?

Manufacturer warranties vary by model and registration requirements. Our workmanship is guaranteed. We review warranty details and documentation with you at installation.

How do I get a free quote for AC installation?

Call (704) 968-1204 for a free quote, or use our website form to request an in-home assessment. We confirm details and provide upfront pricing before work begins.
