Comprehensive AC Tune Up in OKC for Optimal Cooling.

An annual AC tune up in OKC is the key to keeping your air conditioning system running efficiently and reliably all summer long. At Cooper Heat & Air, our comprehensive tune-up service is designed to prevent unexpected breakdowns, improve energy efficiency, and extend the lifespan of your unit. Don't wait for a problem to arise; proactive maintenance saves you money and stress.

Our certified technicians will perform a thorough inspection and cleaning of your entire AC system. We check refrigerant levels, clean coils, inspect electrical components, and ensure every part of your system is in optimal condition. Regular tune-ups can lower your energy bills and improve your home's air quality.

What's Included in Our AC Tune-Up?

  • Comprehensive System Inspection: We check all components for wear and tear.
  • Coil Cleaning: Dirty coils can reduce efficiency; we'll clean them thoroughly.
  • Refrigerant Level Check: We ensure your system has the proper amount of refrigerant for optimal cooling.
  • Electrical Component Inspection: We tighten connections and check for any potential hazards.
  • Thermostat Calibration: We make sure your thermostat is accurately reading and controlling the temperature.
  • Air Filter Replacement: A clean filter is crucial for airflow and air quality.

Serving Oklahoma City, OK and surrounding areas, Cooper Heat & Air is your trusted partner for keeping your AC in top shape.

How often should I get an AC tune-up?

To ensure your system can handle a hot Oklahoma summer without failing, we recommend a professional tune-up every spring. This preventative maintenance is the best way to catch small issues before they turn into costly, mid-July breakdowns.

What are the benefits of a regular AC tune-up?

The main benefits of a professional AC tune up in OKC are lower energy bills and fewer surprise breakdowns. A clean, well-maintained system runs more efficiently, lasts longer, and provides better cooling, making it a small investment that pays for itself.

How long does an AC tune-up take?

Our comprehensive tune-up usually takes 60-90 minutes. This includes cleaning the indoor and outdoor coils, checking refrigerant levels, testing electrical components, and calibrating the system for peak performance.

Will a tune-up make my AC colder?

Often, yes. Dirty coils and incorrect refrigerant levels are common reasons an AC struggles to cool properly. By cleaning key components and verifying system pressures, we can help restore its cooling power and efficiency.

Is an AC tune-up worth the cost?

Definitely. An annual tune-up is a preventative measure that costs far less than an emergency repair call on a hot weekend. It also helps lower your monthly energy bills and can add years to the life of your equipment.

What if you find a problem during the tune-up?

If we find a worn part or a potential issue, we'll show you what we found and explain your options. We provide a clear, upfront price for any recommended repairs and will never perform additional work without your explicit approval.

How do I schedule an AC tune-up?

Scheduling is simple. You can contact us online or call our office at +1 (405) 397-6654. We'll find a convenient time that works for you to get your AC tune up in OKC.
