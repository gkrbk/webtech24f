How does Republic Air Conditioning and Heating price jobs?

We use upfront, flat-rate pricing—no hourly surprises. After diagnosis, you’ll get clear options with firm totals before any work begins. We focus on long-term fixes, not quick upsells.

What’s your process for new customers?

Call or book online, and we’ll schedule a convenient arrival window. Your technician confirms when en route, performs a full system diagnostic, explains the issue in plain language, and reviews repair vs. replace options. Most repairs are completed the same visit. For installs, we perform proper load calculations and handle permits.

Which areas do you serve around Phoenix?

We serve the Greater Phoenix metro, including Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, Glendale, Chandler, Gilbert, Peoria, Surprise, Avondale, Goodyear, and nearby communities.

Why choose Republic Air Conditioning and Heating?

We are a trusted, locally operated business that is fully licensed and insured. We service all major brands and stand behind our work with strong parts-and-labor warranties. Expect honest recommendations and code-compliant workmanship.

Do you offer emergency HVAC service?

Yes. We provide 24/7 emergency service across the Phoenix area for AC and heating breakdowns. We prioritize no-cool/no-heat calls to restore comfort fast.

How do I get a quote or financing/maintenance options?
