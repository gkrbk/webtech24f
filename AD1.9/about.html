How we’ve earned Tulsa’s trust over 22+ years of HVAC service in Tulsa, OK

At Air Dynamics of Tulsa, we’re a father-and-son, family-owned contractor founded in 2003. Patrick and Steven lead a small, highly trained team that pairs precise diagnostics with code-compliant workmanship. As a Ruud Authorized Dealer who services all major brands, we bring factory-informed practices to every job—whether it’s a tune-up, repair, or full system replacement. Our approach is simple: clear options, flat-rate pricing, and respectful service backed by licensing, insurance, and 24/7 availability when Tulsa weather won’t wait.

What “professional & local” means at Air Dynamics

Being local isn’t just an address—it’s accountability. Here’s how we operate:

  • Upfront, flat-rate options after a proper diagnostic—no pressure and no surprises.
  • Right-sized solutions using load calculations and manufacturer specs, with permits and code compliance handled end-to-end.

Expert heating & cooling—tailored to your home or small business
