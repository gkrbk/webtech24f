What’s Included in a Quality Commercial HVAC Replacement

A reliable commercial install begins with planning and ends with verified performance. We focus on correct sizing, safe connections, and a clean, code‑compliant changeout that supports efficiency and uptime.

Load Assessment & Right‑Sizing

We evaluate building use, envelope, and ventilation to select tonnage and configuration that meet comfort and energy goals.

Crane & Access Coordination

For rooftops and tight sites, we plan lifts, safety, and access windows to minimize disruption to your business.

Ductwork & Airflow Review

We review supply/return paths and balance considerations so the new system delivers even temperatures and quiet operation.

Refrigeration Piping & Charge

We size, braze, pressure test, evacuate, and charge to manufacturer specifications for dependable performance.

Electrical, Controls & Safety

We set and level equipment, make electrical connections to code, integrate controls, and verify safeties and sensors.

Start‑Up & Commissioning

We confirm airflow, temperatures, staging, and communication, then review basic operation so your team knows what to expect.

Commercial HVAC Replacement FAQs

Commercial HVAC Replacement in Tulsa: What to Know

Contact Us

How much does a commercial HVAC replacement cost in Tulsa?

Cost depends on building size, unit type and tonnage, electrical and gas needs, curb or stand requirements, ductwork, controls, and access/lift complexity. We assess the site and then provide a clear, upfront price for the full changeout. You approve the scope and cost before work begins.

How long does a commercial replacement take?

Timelines vary by scope. Standard like‑for‑like replacements can often be completed in about a day once equipment and crane access are set. Projects needing duct, electrical, gas, or control changes may take longer. After the site visit, we outline the expected schedule.

How do you size and select the right unit?

We evaluate load, occupancy, ventilation, and duct capacity. The goal is even temperatures and steady operation without short cycling. We review options that fit your building, budget, and comfort needs before ordering.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Where required, we coordinate permits and inspections. We install to local code and manufacturer specifications, including electrical, gas, venting, clearance, and condensate requirements.

What should we expect on replacement day?

We stage equipment, protect access paths, remove the old unit, set and level the new equipment, make electrical and refrigeration connections, pressure test, evacuate, and charge. We complete start‑up and confirm system operation before we leave.

Will this improve efficiency and comfort?

Newer equipment often delivers better efficiency, staging, and controls. Results depend on sizing, duct condition, and setup. We configure the system to support even temperatures and efficient operation.

How do we schedule a commercial replacement estimate?

Call (918) 728-9420 or send a request through our website. We’ll arrange a site visit in the Tulsa area, answer questions, and provide a clear written estimate for your commercial HVAC replacement.
