Common Furnace Problems We Fix:

  • Furnace Won't Turn On: We check for issues with the thermostat, power supply, or pilot light.
  • Not Enough Heat: This can be caused by a clogged filter, faulty blower motor, or other issues.
  • Strange Noises: Rattling, banging, or squealing sounds can indicate mechanical problems that need immediate attention.
  • Frequent Cycling: A furnace that turns on and off too often may have a clogged filter or a faulty thermostat.
  • Yellow Pilot Light: A yellow or flickering pilot light can indicate a carbon monoxide leak and should be addressed immediately.

What are the signs that my furnace needs repair?

Common signs include loud banging or squealing noises, a pilot light that's yellow instead of blue, or rooms that just won't get warm. If your furnace cycles on and off constantly or your heating bills spike, it's time to call for a professional furnace repair in OKC.

How quickly can you respond to a furnace repair call?

We know having no heat is an emergency. We prioritize repair calls and offer same-day service whenever possible to get your heat back on. Our trucks are stocked with common parts to resolve most issues in a single visit.

Do you repair all brands of furnaces?

Yes. Our certified technicians are trained to service and repair all major makes and models of gas and electric furnaces. We have the expertise to diagnose and fix the problem correctly, regardless of your system's brand.

Should I repair or replace my furnace?

We'll give you an honest assessment. For newer furnaces with minor issues, a repair is best. If your furnace is over 15 years old or requires a major, expensive part, replacement is often the more reliable and cost-effective long-term solution.

What should I do if I smell gas near my furnace?

If you smell gas, do not touch any lights or electronics. Leave your home immediately, and from a safe distance, call your gas company's emergency line. Once they have declared the area safe, call us to inspect your furnace.

Are your furnace repairs guaranteed?

Absolutely. We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty on both parts and labor. Our goal is to provide a safe, reliable, and lasting repair, not just a temporary fix.
