Expert Diagnostics & Repair

Common AC Problems We Fix in Tulsa

From a sudden system failure to strange noises, our expert technicians have the experience to accurately diagnose and resolve any AC issue. We handle all common and complex problems to restore your comfort fast.

AC Not Turning On

A dead air conditioner can be caused by a tripped breaker, a faulty thermostat, or a blown capacitor. We'll trace the issue to its source and safely restore power to your system.

AC Blowing Warm Air

If your AC is running but not cooling, the cause could be a refrigerant leak, a dirty evaporator coil, or a broken compressor. We'll find the issue and fix it right.

Strange Noises or Smells

Buzzing or rattling sounds often point to a failing fan motor or loose parts, while musty smells can indicate mold. We'll identify the source and resolve it safely.

Refrigerant Leaks

Low refrigerant can damage your compressor. We are EPA certified to handle refrigerant, allowing us to expertly find and fix leaks, then recharge your system to exact specifications.

Thermostat Malfunctions

A faulty thermostat can cause your system to cycle improperly or fail to turn on. We can recalibrate, repair, or upgrade your thermostat to restore precise climate control.

AC Leaking Water

Pooling water is often a clogged condensate drain line, which can cause significant damage. We'll clear the blockage and ensure proper drainage to protect your home.
