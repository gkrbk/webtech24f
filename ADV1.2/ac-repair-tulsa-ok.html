Signs that your air conditioner or HVAC system might need a repair:

  • Warm Air Blowing from Vents: A clear indicator your system isn't cooling properly.
  • Strange Noises: Rattling, banging, squealing, or hissing can signal serious issues.
  • Weak Airflow: Significant decreases in airflow may point to clogged filters, leaks, or other problems.
  • High Energy Bills: A sudden spike in costs without increased usage can mean your unit is working too hard.
  • Inconsistent Cooling: Some rooms being warmer or cooler could indicate duct or airflow concerns.
  • Frequent Cycling: Rapid on-and-off cycles might mean your system is struggling to maintain the set temperature.
  • Water Leaks: Leaks around the unit suggest refrigerant or drainage issues.
  • Burning Smell: Electrical odors must be addressed immediately to prevent damage or danger.
  • Age of the Unit: Systems over 10 years old may need more frequent repairs or a complete replacement.
  • Thermostat Issues: A thermostat that won't respond or maintain temperature could be part of the problem.

What are the most common signs my AC system needs repair?

Watch for grinding or squealing noises, weak airflow from vents, musty smells, or water leaking around the indoor unit. If your AC runs constantly but can't keep up, it's time to schedule a professional air conditioner repair in OKC.

How quickly can Cooper Heat & Air respond to AC repair requests in OKC?

We know a broken AC is an emergency in an Oklahoma summer. We offer same-day service whenever possible to diagnose the problem and get your home cool again quickly. Our trucks are stocked with common parts to complete most repairs in a single visit.

Do you repair all brands and types of AC systems?

Yes. Our NATE-certified technicians have the experience and tools to service all major makes and models of central air conditioners, heat pumps, and ductless mini-split systems.

Will I get an upfront estimate before AC repairs begin?

Absolutely. Once our technician identifies the problem, we'll explain the needed repair and provide a clear, flat-rate price. You approve the work and the cost before we start, so there are no surprises.

Should I repair or replace my air conditioner?

We'll give you an honest recommendation. If your system is older (10+ years) and needs a major, expensive part like a compressor, replacement might be a better long-term investment. For newer systems with minor issues, a repair is usually the most sensible option.

Are your AC repairs guaranteed?

Yes. We are committed to providing reliable, lasting solutions, not just quick fixes. We stand behind our work with a full warranty on both parts and labor for your peace of mind.
