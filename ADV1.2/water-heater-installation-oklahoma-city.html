How do I know if I need a new water heater?

Any visible leaks are an immediate sign. Other red flags include rusty or discolored hot water, rumbling or popping noises from the tank, or simply not getting enough hot water. If your unit is over 10 years old, it's wise to plan for a replacement.

Will a new water heater lower my energy bills?

Yes. Modern water heaters are far more energy-efficient than models from a decade ago, which can lead to significant savings on your monthly utility bills. High-efficiency tankless models provide the greatest savings by only heating water when you need it.

What's the difference between a tank and a tankless water heater?

A traditional tank heater keeps a large reserve of water hot 24/7. A tankless heater heats water instantly on demand, providing an endless supply. We can help you decide which type best fits your family's hot water usage, budget, and home layout.

How long does a water heater installation take?

A standard replacement of a tank-style water heater typically takes 2 to 4 hours. Converting to a tankless system or relocating the unit can take longer. We'll give you a clear timeline with your estimate.

Do you offer financing for new water heaters?

Yes, we offer several flexible financing options to make your new water heater more affordable. We can walk you through the simple application process and help you find a plan that works for your budget.

Does Cooper Heat & Air offer warranties on new installations?

Absolutely. Your new water heater is protected by a strong manufacturer's warranty, and we fully guarantee the quality of our own workmanship. We ensure every unit is installed to code for safe, reliable, long-term performance.
